Our Faculty

QC Business Career Advisors

Diane Shults is currently Associate Director of Alumni & Employer Engagement at Queens College. She works with Alumni and Employers to bring career opportunities to students. Diane also works as a Career Educator with students in Economics, Business, Accounting & Risk Management on career competencies to prepare them for the transition from College to Career. Prior to QC, Diane worked in Human Resources for several financial institutions, such as Credit Suisse, Citi and Bank of America.

Eleanor Mavashev is a Business Career Educator & Internship Coordinator at Queens College Center for Career Engagement and Internships. Eleanor works alongside Diane Shults, Associate Director of Alumni & Employer Engagement, by assisting business students with career guidance and securing internship/job opportunities. Prior to Queens College, Eleanor worked at mid-size accounting firm, hospitality area and at Long Island University as a Career Counselor. In addition, she also runs her own tax practice.
